## 🔥 Bake Workflow

1. High-poly sculpt complete
2. Retopo to low-poly
3. UV unwrap low-poly
4. Match cage (offset 0.05-0.1)
5. Bake in Marmoset Toolbag:
   - Normal (tangent space)
   - AO
   - Curvature
   - Position
   - ID map
6. Import to Substance Painter
7. Apply smart materials with baked masks

## 💡 Engine-Specific Notes

- Lumen lighting
- Substance Live link
- Material instances for variants

- GLB compressed (Draco)
- Max 2 materials
- Texture < 1024 (mobile)
- Polycount < 50K total scene
