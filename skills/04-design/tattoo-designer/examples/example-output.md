### Why this placement
1. **Profession-friendly** — long sleeve hide, short sleeve shows artistic side ตอน weekend
2. **First big piece** — outer arm pain level low (3-5/10) → easier first experience
3. **Body curve** — phoenix bird wings can wrap around bicep dynamically
4. **Aging well** — upper arm = low sun + low friction = preserve detail 30+ years
5. **Future expansion** — can extend to full sleeve down to wrist later

### Stencil orientation
- Phoenix HEAD points UPWARD toward shoulder
- Phoenix tail flows DOWN toward elbow
- Sunflowers cluster at center (bicep peak — most visible point)
- Clouds + waves wrap around to inner arm (less detail = less pain)

## 🩹 After-Care Guide (สำคัญมาก สำหรับ piece ใหญ่)

### Day 1-3 (Critical)
- ✅ Keep wrap (Saniderm/Tegaderm) on 24-48 hr ตามแม่ instructions
- ✅ Wash gentle: cold water + fragrance-free soap (Dr. Bronner's Baby)
- ✅ Pat dry with paper towel (no fabric — fiber stick)
- ✅ Thin layer Hustle Butter Deluxe (vegan healing balm)
- ✅ Sleep on opposite side (left side — protect right arm)
- ⚠️ **Expect:** redness, swelling, weeping plasma — normal

### Day 4-14 (Healing - peak itch period!)
- ✅ Wash 2-3 times/day gentle
- ✅ Apply Hustle Butter or Cetaphil 4-5 times/day (skin = thirsty)
- ⚠️ **DO NOT:**
  - แกะ scab (จะทำให้ ink หาย — color จะ patchy)
  - ขีดข่วน (ถึงจะคันมาก)
  - Submerge: NO bath, NO swimming, NO hot tub
  - Direct sun: cover always
  - Tight clothing: loose t-shirt only
- ✅ ใส่ loose cotton tank top
- ⚠️ **Itch period (day 7-12)** — ตบเบาๆ ไม่ scratch

### Day 14-30 (Settling - looks dull)
- ✅ Continue moisturize 2-3 times/day
- ⚠️ Tattoo จะดู "milky" + dull — **ปกติ** (skin ลอกใต้ surface)
- ✅ Color จะกลับมา bright หลัง full heal (week 4-6)
- ⚠️ ห้าม intense sun (covered always)
- ✅ Resume gym week 3 (no direct contact area)

### Day 30+ (Fully healed)
- ✅ Apply SPF 30+ before any sun exposure (lifetime!)
- ✅ Moisturize daily (skin = canvas)
- ✅ Touch-up appointment booked week 19 (post session 3)
- ❌ Tan beds = devastating to tattoo color
- ❌ Excessive long shower (warm water fade)

### Long-term (annual care)
- 🌞 SPF 30+ always when sun > 30 min exposure
- 🧴 Moisturize tattoo area daily

## 🚨 Warning Signs (Seek Doctor)

### Normal (don't panic)
- Redness around tattoo: first 3 days
- Plasma weeping: first 24 hr
- Itching: day 7-14
- Mild swelling: first 48 hr
- Scab forming: week 1-2
- Dull/milky look: week 2-4

### EMERGENCY → ไป hospital ทันที
- 🔴 Spreading redness > 5 cm beyond tattoo
- 🤢 Pus discharge (yellow/green/foul smell)
- 🌡️ Fever > 38°C
- 💥 Severe swelling 3+ days
- 🔴 **Red streaks** radiating from tattoo (sepsis risk!)
- 😵 Allergic rash (hives, breathing trouble) — esp. after red ink

### Specific to red ink allergy
- Bumps + itch only on RED area (not whole tattoo)
- Persistent itch 1+ month after heal
- → Consult dermatologist (may need laser remove red part)
