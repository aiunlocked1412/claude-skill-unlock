## 🩹 After-Care Guide

### Day 1-3 (Critical)
- Keep wrap on 2-4 hr after leaving studio (artist instructions)
- Wash gentle with cold water + fragrance-free soap
- Pat dry with paper towel (no fabric — fiber stick)
- Thin layer of healing balm (Aquaphor, Hustle Butter, Bepanthen)
- Sleep on opposite side (don't crush tattoo)

### Day 4-14 (Healing)
- Wash 2-3 times/day gentle
- Apply fragrance-free moisturizer (Cetaphil, Eucerin)
- ⚠️ DO NOT pick / scratch / peel scab
- ⚠️ DO NOT submerge (no swimming, no bath, no hot tub)
- ⚠️ DO NOT direct sun (covered always)
- Wear loose clothing over area

### Day 14-30 (Settling)
- Continue moisturize daily
- Tattoo will look "milky" then bright again
- Avoid intense sun
- Resume gym after 2 weeks (gentle first)

### Forever
- ✅ SPF 30+ before sun exposure
- ✅ Moisturize regularly (skin = canvas)
- ❌ Tan beds = devastating
- ❌ Excessive water (long shower fade)

## 🚨 Warning Signs (Seek Doctor)

ถ้าเจอ:
- 🔴 Spreading redness > 5 cm from tattoo
- 🤢 Pus + foul smell (yellow/green discharge)
- 🌡️ Fever > 38°C
- 💥 Severe swelling 3+ days
- 🔴 Red streaks radiating from tattoo (sepsis sign!)
- 😵 Allergic rash (hives, breathing trouble)

→ ไป hospital ทันที (อย่ารอ)

## ⚠️ Important Warnings

### Permanent decision
- Tattoo อยู่ตลอดชีวิต — ลบยาก/แพง (laser ฿5K/session × 6-15 sessions)
- Touch-up หลัง 5-10 ปี ปกติ (color fade)
- Aging body = tattoo distort (pregnancy, weight change)

### Profession check
- Visible tattoo อาจกระทบ:
  - Thai government employee
  - Military / Police
  - Hotel front office (some chains)
  - Flight attendant (Asian airlines stricter)
  - Conservative office Bangkok
- Hidden placement = safer career-wise

### Future regret prevention
- ❌ ห้าม partner's name (separation #1 regret)
- ❌ ห้าม trending meme (outdate ใน 2-5 ปี)
- ❌ ห้าม impulse decision (sleep on it 1 month minimum)
- ❌ ห้าม tattoo while drunk / high

### Cultural respect
- Hindu deities → ห้ามใต้เอว (offensive)
- Buddha image → ห้ามขา/ฝ่าเท้า (offensive ใน Thailand law)
- Maori/Indigenous patterns → consult elder
- Sak Yant (ยันต์ไทย) → ทำที่วัด with Ajarn (proper)
