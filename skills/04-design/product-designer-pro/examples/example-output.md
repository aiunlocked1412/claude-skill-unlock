## 📐 Design Spec

### Drip Sachet (Primary Package)

**Product Dimensions:**
- Flat sachet: 120mm × 85mm × 4mm (flat)
- Material: 4-layer laminate film
- Weight (empty): 2.5g
- Fill capacity: 15g ground coffee (medium-coarse)
- Gross weight: 17.5g

**Structure:**
- 3-side sealed sachet (top, left, right)
- Perforated tear line at top (easy open, no scissors needed)
- Drip bag inside: hanging paper filter (food-grade, chlorine-free)
- Filter hooks: 2 paper flaps that hook over coffee cup rim

**Print Specification:**
- Print method: Rotogravure (higher quality for small-batch premium)
- Colors: CMYK + 2 spot colors (Pantone 476 C espresso brown + Pantone 872 C copper foil)
- Finish: matte lamination (whole surface) + copper hot-foil stamp on logo
- Emboss: subtle emboss on "DRIP" typography (adds tactile premium cue)
- Pantone colors:
  - `#3A2E1F` (Espresso Bean) — Pantone 476 C
  - `#B8956C` (Copper Foil) — Pantone 872 C foil
  - `#F5EFE6` (Cream) — stock kraft paper base color

**Dieline Notes:**
- Bleed: 3mm all sides
- Safety zone: 5mm from cut
- Tear notch: 8mm from top, V-shape 2mm deep
- Barcode zone: bottom-right 30×20mm white area

## 🧱 Material Plan

### Sachet Material
- **Composition:** PET 12µ / AL 7µ / LDPE 80µ laminate (standard barrier for coffee)
- **Source:** PTT Polymer Thailand (local supplier, lower MOQ)
- **Sustainability note:** Traditional laminate is NOT recyclable. Add QR code → recycling program (collect back for 5% discount next order)
- **Cost estimate:** 2.50 THB per sachet at 15,000 units
- **Alternative (future):** Compostable PLA laminate (Taghleef Industries) — +80% cost, but 100% compostable ASTM D6400

### 10-Pack Box
- **Material:** FSC-certified kraft paperboard 350gsm
- **Source:** SCG Paper Thailand
- **Sustainability:** Fully recyclable (paper stream), FSC Mix certification

## 💡 Designer Notes

**Why this design works:**
1. **Origin-first storytelling** — "Ethiopian Yirgacheffe" is the hero, not generic "Coffee"
2. **Premium tactile cues** — matte kraft + copper foil + emboss = 3 sensory premium cues
3. **Transparency** — QR code to actual farm story builds trust + differentiation
4. **Sustainability layering** — FSC paperboard + seed paper cards + take-back program = authentic eco
5. **Unboxing as marketing** — designed to drive UGC content

**Tradeoffs / concerns:**
- **Laminate film not recyclable** — biggest sustainability weakness. Mitigated by take-back program, but honest about limitation on packaging
- **Copper foil MOQ** — requires 15,000 min units to be economical (first batch commitment)
- **Letterpress + seed paper** — adds 4 THB to unboxing, only feasible for direct e-commerce orders (not resellers)

**Future evolution:**
- Year 2: Compostable PLA laminate when cost drops
- Year 2: Branded drip coffee station (gift box + ceramic server)
- Year 3: Limited edition "Farmer series" — each harvest season different farm
