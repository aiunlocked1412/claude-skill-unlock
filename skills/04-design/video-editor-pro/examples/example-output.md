## 🎨 Color Grading Plan

**Style:** Kodak Portra 400 film emulation (Dehancer plugin)

**LUT base:** Kodak Portra 400 at 60% opacity + custom adjustments

**Primary Correction (per shot):**
- Match exposure across 4K A7S III + FX3 clips (different ISO handling)
- WB target: 5200-5500K (slight warm bias)
- Highlight recovery: -20

**Color Wheels:**
- Shadows: Hue 220 (blue), Sat 18, Lift +0.02 (slightly lifted/faded)
- Midtones: Hue 30, Sat 8 (warm skin)
- Highlights: Hue 45 (golden), Sat 15, Gain +0.05

**HSL adjustments:**
- Red: hue +5, lum +10 (warmer skin, brighter)
- Orange (skin): hue -5, sat -5, lum +12 (flatter clean skin)
- Yellow: sat -25 (muted yellows, no cartoon)
- Green: sat -30, hue +5 (olive/teal green)
- Blue: sat -10 (muted sky)

**Finishing:**
- Vignette: -0.25 edge soft
- Film grain: 10% 35mm Kodak profile
- Film halation: 15% (subtle red glow on highlights)
- Sharpening: +10 (light, film has softness)

**Scene-specific override:**
- Scene 4 (golden hour): push highlights Hue 50, Sat 25 (extra glow)
- Scene 5 (outro): increase shadow density -0.05 (moodier black)
