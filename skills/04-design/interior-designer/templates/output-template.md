## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Climate adaptation (Thailand context)
- Use AC-friendly material (avoid heat-trap fabric)
- Curtain block UV (west-facing window)
- Choose moisture-resistant in bathroom area
- Ventilation for closed wardrobe (mold prevention)

### Maintenance tips
- Linen requires gentle wash (cold, low spin)
- Brass develops patina (love it or polish weekly)
- White oak need oil refresh annually
- Limewash paint touch-up easy (mix water)

### Resale consideration
- Neutral palette = +10% resale value
- Avoid permanent bold paint (use accent wall instead)
- Quality kitchen + bathroom = highest ROI
