## Gem Reference (4Cs + Hardness)

### Diamond (Mohs 10)
| Cut shape | Best for | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Round Brilliant | Max fire | Most popular (60%), most expensive per ct |
| Princess | Modern square | Sharp corner = chip risk |
| Oval | Elongating finger | Affordable alternative to round |
| Cushion | Vintage romantic | Pillow shape, soft corner |
| Emerald | Art deco | Step cut = clarity must be high |
| Pear | Drop | Wear with point toward nail |
| Marquise | Boat | Maximizes carat illusion |
| Asscher | Art deco square | Like emerald cut + extra facets |
| Radiant | Modern brilliant | Cushion shape + brilliant cut faceting |
| Heart | Romantic | Hard to cut well |

### Color grade (D-Z scale)
- **D-F** Colorless ($$$$$)
- **G-J** Near colorless (sweet spot value)
- **K-M** Faint warm
- **N-Z** Visible color (avoid for engagement)

### Clarity grade
- **FL/IF** Flawless ($$$$$ rare)
- **VVS1/2** Very very slight inclusion (eye-clean, $$$$)
- **VS1/2** Very slight (eye-clean ~95%, $$$)
- **SI1/2** Slight inclusion (eye-clean ~50%, $$)
- **I1-3** Included (visible flaw, $)

### Colored Gemstones

| Stone | Mohs | Best origin | Notes |
|-------|------|-------------|-------|
| Sapphire | 9 | Kashmir, Burma, Ceylon | Blue most valued, Padparadscha rare |
| Ruby | 9 | Burma "pigeon blood", Mozambique | Red sapphire = ruby |
| Emerald | 7.5-8 | Colombia (Muzo), Zambia | Always has "jardin" inclusion |
| Tanzanite | 6.5-7 | Tanzania only | Soft, occasion only |
| Tsavorite | 6.5-7.5 | Kenya/Tanzania green garnet | Emerald alternative |
| Spinel | 8 | Burma red | Long mistaken for ruby |
| Tourmaline | 7-7.5 | Brazil (paraiba blue) | Wide color range |
| Aquamarine | 7.5-8 | Brazil | Blue beryl, light/eye-clean |
| Morganite | 7.5-8 | Brazil | Pink beryl, trendy 2020s |
| Opal | 5.5-6.5 | Australia | Soft, scratches easily |
| Pearl | 2.5-4.5 | Akoya/Tahitian/South Sea | Care: store soft, no chemicals |
| Onyx | 6.5-7 | Brazil | Black opaque, classic |
| Jade | 6-7 | Burma jadeite, China nephrite | Imperial green most valued |

### Treatment disclosure
- **None / Natural / Untreated** — premium
- **Heat (H)** — common in sapphire/ruby, accepted norm
- **Be diffusion** — controversial, must disclose
- **Oil (E)** — emerald standard (Cedar oil)
- **Glass-filled** — affects value heavily
- **HPHT / Lab-grown** — must disclose, 70-90% cheaper

### Certification labs (trust order)
1. GIA — global gold standard
2. AGS — cut specialist
3. GRS — colored stone (Swiss)
4. Gübelin — premium colored stone
5. SSEF — Swiss premium
6. AIGS — Bangkok-based Thai standard

## Metal Reference

### Gold purity
| Karat | % Gold | Use case | Color |
|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| 24K (999) | 99.9% | Investment, ceremonial | Yellow pure |
| 22K (916) | 91.6% | Indian style | Yellow rich |
| 18K (750) | 75.0% | Premium standard | All colors |
| 14K (585) | 58.5% | US/EU common | All colors |
| 10K (417) | 41.7% | Budget (US min) | Pale |
| 9K (375) | 37.5% | UK budget min | Pale |

### Gold color alloy
| Color | Alloy mix | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Yellow | Au + Cu + Ag (equal) | Classic warm |
| White | Au + Pd or Ni (rhodium plate) | Modern, replate every 2 yr |
| Rose | Au + Cu (more Cu = rosier) | Trendy 2020s, suits warm skin |
| Green | Au + Ag (no Cu) | Rare, antique |

### Platinum (Pt)
- Pt950 — 95% pure (5% Ir or Ru)
- Heaviest noble metal (1 ring = 7-15g vs 5-10g gold)
- Hypoallergenic (no nickel)
- Develops patina (love it or buff)
- 30% more expensive than gold (per gram)

### Silver
| Type | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Sterling 925 | Standard, tarnishes (clean weekly) |
| Argentium 935+ | Anti-tarnish, slightly white |
| Fine Silver 999 | Too soft, only filigree |

### Other
- **Palladium 950** — white, lighter than Pt, hypoallergenic
- **Titanium / Tungsten** — men's modern, can't be resized

## Lighting for Render

### Studio product
- Soft box overhead + side
- Background: white seamless / black velvet / grey marble / silk
- Key: 5500K daylight LED
- Shadow: soft (use diffuser)

### Macro detail (gem fire)
- Single point light from upper-left
- Showcase fire/scintillation
- Black background = max contrast

### Lifestyle on hand
- Soft natural window light
- Bokeh background
- Hand model with manicure
- Slight motion blur for elegance

## Common Pitfalls

❌ Eternity ring ที่ resize ไม่ได้ → daily wear ปัญหา (finger size เปลี่ยน 5%)
❌ Soft stone (opal, pearl) บน engagement ring → ปริ
❌ White gold ไม่ replate → กลับเหลืองหลัง 2 ปี
❌ Sterling silver ที่ไม่บอกว่า tarnish (จุดดำ ไม่ใช่ defect)
❌ ลืมระบุ treatment ของ gem (legal issue)
❌ Photo gem วิทยา macro แต่ไม่ระบุ retouch (จริงๆ ตาเปล่าไม่ shiny ขนาดนั้น)
❌ ใช้ "diamond simulant" คำคลุมเครือ (CZ, moissanite ต้องบอกตรงๆ)

## Care & Maintenance Recommend (always include)

- Ultrasonic clean ทุก 6 เดือน (ไม่ใช้กับ pearl, opal, emerald)
- Soft cloth wipe หลัง wear (sweat = tarnish)
- เก็บแยก pouch (กันรอย scratch)
- ห้ามใส่ตอนทำสวน, ออกกำลังกาย, อาบน้ำคลอรีน
- Inspection ทุกปี (check prong + stone secure)
- Insurance > ฿100K = recommend
