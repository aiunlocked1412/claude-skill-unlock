## Packing Checklist by Climate

### Cold (0-15°C)
- Down jacket / heavy coat
- Heat tech inner layer (shirt + pants)
- Wool sweater
- Beanie + gloves + scarf
- Boots non-slip
- Fleece-lined socks
- Lip balm (severe dryness)

### Mild (16-22°C)
- Light jacket / cardigan
- Long sleeve + jeans
- Sneakers
- Light scarf
- Umbrella

### Warm (23-28°C)
- T-shirt + light pants/shorts
- Sneakers + sandals
- Sun hat
- Sunglasses

### Hot (29°C+)
- Light cotton/linen
- Quick-dry clothes
- Sandals + flip-flops
- Wide-brim hat
- Cooling towel
- Sunblock SPF50+

### Beach/Water
- Swimsuit (2 sets)
- Rash guard
- Snorkel mask
- Reef-safe sunblock
- Dry bag
- Aqua shoes

## Universal Items

- Passport + 6 months validity
- Visa printed + e-copy
- Booking confirmations
- Travel insurance certificate
- Emergency contact list (Thai embassy + family)
- Cash USD ($200-500) + 2 cards
- Phone + portable charger 10,000mAh
- Universal adapter
- Medication (with prescription if RX)
- First aid: pain killer, anti-diarrhea, plaster, antiseptic
- Sunglasses + sunblock + lip balm
- Personal hygiene mini
