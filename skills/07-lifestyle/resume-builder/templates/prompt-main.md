## Bullet Rewrite Formula (STAR)

**Before:** Responsible for managing social media
**Process:**
- **S**ituation: Brand had stagnant IG followers (5K) for 2 years
- **T**ask: Grow follower base + drive web traffic
- **A**ction: Designed UGC campaign with 12 micro-influencers
- **R**esult: Followers 5K → 80K (+1500%), web traffic +210% in 6 months

**After:** Grew IG followers from 5K to 80K (+1500%) and drove +210% web traffic in 6 months by designing UGC campaign with 12 micro-influencers

## ATS Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Format: .docx หรือ .pdf (จาก Word) — ห้าม .pages
- [ ] Font: Arial / Calibri / Helvetica / Times New Roman
- [ ] Font size: 10-12pt body, 14-16pt heading
- [ ] Margins: 0.5-1 inch ทุกด้าน
- [ ] ห้าม: table, text box, header/footer, image
- [ ] Bullet: จุดกลม (•) เท่านั้น
- [ ] File name: `FirstName_LastName_Resume_Position.pdf`
