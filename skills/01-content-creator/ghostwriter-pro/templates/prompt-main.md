# Ghostwriter Templates — Voice, Byline, Ethics, Billing

## Voice Analysis Framework (7 Dimensions)

### Dimension 1: Sentence Length
- **Short (5-10 words avg):** punchy, decisive — Steve Jobs, Hemingway
- **Medium (12-20):** balanced, conversational — most LinkedIn execs
- **Long (25+):** academic, complex — Ezra Klein, McKinsey thought leaders
- **Mixed:** varied for rhythm — most great writers

**How to detect:** Count words in 20 random sentences from samples.

### Dimension 2: Vocabulary Register
| Register | Marker words |
|----------|--------------|
| Formal | "however", "moreover", "thus", "facilitate" |
| Professional | "build", "ship", "scale", "leverage" |
| Casual | "kinda", "tbh", "vibes", "gonna" |
| Industry slang | "TAM", "MOAT", "unit economics" |

**Mix matters:** บางคนมี base professional + spike casual ตอนจบ post = signature

### Dimension 3: Pet Phrases (Idiosyncrasy)
หา phrase ที่ลูกค้าใช้ซ้ำ — มักไม่รู้ตัว:
- "Here's the thing —"
- "ผมว่าเรื่องนี้น่าสนใจตรงที่..."
- "TL;DR:"
- "Hot take:"
- "นี่คือสิ่งที่ผมเรียนรู้..."

ใช้ 1-2 ในแต่ละ piece — fingerprint ของ voice

### Dimension 4: Humor Type
- **Dry / observational:** "Investors say 'product-market fit' the way other people say 'I love you' — usually too late"
- **Self-deprecating:** "ผมเป็น CEO ที่ห่วยที่สุดของบริษัทตัวเอง — โชคดีที่ทีมเก่ง"
- **Roast (industry):** sharper, riskier
- **None:** corporate professional, ไม่มีก็ไม่มี

### Dimension 5: Story Device
- **Anecdote-first:** เปิดด้วย story เสมอ — Ali Abdaal, Tim Ferriss
- **Data-first:** เปิดด้วยตัวเลข/research — Lenny, Stratechery
- **Metaphor-first:** เปิดด้วยภาพเปรียบ — Naval, Morgan Housel
- **Contrarian-first:** เปิดด้วย claim ที่ขัดความเชื่อ — Peter Thiel

### Dimension 6: Pronouns + Voice Distance
- "I" heavy → personal, vulnerable
- "We" heavy → team-oriented, founder/leader voice
- "You" heavy → coaching/teaching voice
- "It / they" heavy → analytical, distanced

**สัดส่วนสำคัญ:** บาง CEO 30% I + 50% we + 20% you = signature mix

### Dimension 7: Opening + Closing Patterns
- เปิด 5 post เก่า ดูว่าใช้ pattern เดียวกันไหม?
- บางคนเปิดด้วย question เสมอ บางคนเปิดด้วย anecdote เสมอ
- Closing: บางคนปิดด้วย question → reader engagement (LinkedIn algo ชอบ)

## Byline Format Specs

### LinkedIn Post (1,000-1,800 chars)
- Hook 1 บรรทัด (cut by "see more" — ต้องน่าคลิก)
- Body 6-12 บรรทัด (each line = 1-2 sentences max)
- White space เยอะ (mobile)
- CTA / question ตอนจบ
- 0-3 hashtag (มากเกิน LinkedIn ลด reach)

### Op-Ed (700-1,200 words)
- Lede paragraph: hook + thesis ใน 100 words
- 3-5 supporting points (each ~150 words)
- Counter-argument acknowledgment 1 paragraph
- Strong close + call to action
- Byline + bio (2-3 lines)

### Keynote Speech (15-min = ~1,800 words)
- Opening hook 90 sec
- Main thesis statement
- 3 stories / examples (4 min each)
- Synthesis + call to action 90 sec
- Mark `[PAUSE]`, `[SLOW]`, `[GESTURE]` cues

### Book Chapter (3,000-6,000 words)
- Chapter hook (story/scene)
- Key concept introduction
- 3-5 supporting sub-sections
- Application / exercise
- Transition to next chapter

## Ghost Agreement Essentials

```markdown
# GHOST WRITING AGREEMENT

## 1. Scope of Work
- Number of pieces: __
- Format: __ (LinkedIn post / blog / book chapter)
- Word count: __ per piece
- Delivery cadence: __

## 2. Voice & Approval
- Writer follows Voice Profile dated __
- Client provides anecdotes via __ (call/email/Slack)
- Revision rounds: 2 included, additional ฿__/round

## 3. Intellectual Property
- All work is "work for hire" — Client owns 100% upon payment
- Writer grants exclusive perpetual license
- Writer retains right to mention "client X" in own portfolio (after disclosure period)

## 4. Confidentiality
- NDA: 24 months from termination
- No public disclosure of relationship without written consent

## 5. Byline / Attribution
- All pieces published under Client byline
- Writer NOT credited publicly
- Disclosure: [None / "Written with team" / Co-author "with X"]

## 6. Payment
- Rate: ฿__ per piece OR ฿__/month retainer
- Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- Late fee: 2%/month after 14 days

## 7. Termination
- Either party may terminate with 30-day notice
- Pro-rated refund for undelivered work
- All delivered work remains Client property

## 8. Warranties
- Writer warrants work is original (no plagiarism)
- Client warrants anecdotes/data shared are true (Writer not liable for fact-check)

## 9. Governing Law
- Thailand / Delaware / [jurisdiction]
```

## Ethical Disclosure Decision Tree

```
Is the byline legally/ethically required to be the actual author?
│
├─ Yes (academic paper, sworn testimony, journalism with named source)
│  → MUST disclose ghost OR don't take the job
│
└─ No (most business content)
   │
   ├─ Is the topic about "I personally did X"?
   │  → Need real anecdotes from client. Don't fabricate.
   │
   ├─ Is it medical/legal advice from a credentialed person?
   │  → Client must review + approve every claim
   │
   └─ General thought leadership / corporate
      → Standard ghosting OK. NDA + voice match. No disclosure needed.
```

## Scope Creep Defenses

**Common scope creep + response:**

| Client says | You respond |
|-------------|-------------|
| "Quick edit, just 1 paragraph" (5th time) | "Sure — that's revision round 3. Per our agreement, ฿X/round" |
| "Can you also do 2 more posts this week?" | "Happy to. Sending updated quote — ฿Y for 2 extras" |
| "I want it shorter / longer" | "We agreed N words. Adjusting now is fine for round 1-2 free" |
| "Write the bio + headline too" | "Those weren't in scope. ฿Z to add" |

**Key:** Be friendly but firm. Reference agreement always.
