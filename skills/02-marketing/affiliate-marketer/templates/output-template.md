
### Attribution Model
- **Standard:** Last-click within cookie window
- **Top tier:** First-click (gives credit to discovery)
- **Cookie duration:** 30d standard, 60-90d top tier
- **Cross-device:** Match by email or logged-in user ID
- **Postback:** Server-to-server fire on order completion

### Refund Window
- Approve commission 14 วันหลัง purchase
- Auto-deduct ถ้า refund ใน window

## 🛡️ Fraud Prevention

| Rule | Detection | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Self-affiliate | IP+email match | Deny commission |
| Cookie stuffing | Impr:click > 1000:1 | Manual review |
| Brand bidding | PPC keyword "<brand>" | Suspend + notice |
| Refund abuse | Partner refund > 30% | Demote tier |
| Velocity spike | 10x normal in 24h | Hold + verify |
| Bot traffic | 0% engagement | Block |
