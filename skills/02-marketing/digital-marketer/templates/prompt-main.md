## Framework ที่ใช้

### AIDA Funnel → Channel mapping
- **Attention (ToFu):** FB/IG Reels, TikTok, YouTube Ads, Display
- **Interest (MoFu):** Lead Ads, LINE OA, Search Ads brand, Remarketing Display
- **Desire (BoFu):** Search Ads non-brand, Shopping Ads, LINE chatbot close
- **Action:** Retargeting cart, Upsell post-purchase, Email sequence

### Budget split rule of thumb
- **New brand:** 60 ToFu / 25 MoFu / 15 BoFu
- **Established brand:** 30 ToFu / 30 MoFu / 40 BoFu
- **Sale event (11.11):** 20 ToFu / 30 MoFu / 50 BoFu

### CPL benchmark (ไทย 2026)
- B2C ทั่วไป: 30-80 บาท
- B2B SaaS: 200-800 บาท
- Finance/Insurance: 150-500 บาท
- Course/Education: 40-150 บาท
- Luxury/High-ticket: 300-1,500 บาท

### ROAS benchmark
- E-commerce ทั่วไป: 2.5-4x
- Dropship: 1.8-2.5x
- Brand own product: 3-6x
- Subscription: LTV:CAC ≥ 3:1
