## Pricing Tier Strategy

### Anchor + Decoy Model

| Tier | Price | Logic |
|------|-------|-------|
| Anchor (VIP) | 3x base | ทำให้ standard ดูถูก |
| Standard | 1x base | Main revenue |
| Early-bird | 0.6x base | Build momentum + cash flow |
| Group (5+) | 0.8x × N | B2B sales |
| Student | 0.4x | Brand + future audience |
| Sponsor | Free | Per package |

### Pricing Math (ตัวอย่าง 500 คน)

```
Capacity: 500
Cost: 800,000 (venue + AV + speaker fee + F&B + marketing)
Target margin: 30%
Required revenue: 1,140,000

Base ticket: 2,500
- Early-bird (40%): 200 × 1,500 = 300,000
- Standard (45%): 225 × 2,500 = 562,500
- VIP (10%): 50 × 5,000 = 250,000
- Group (5%): 25 × 2,000 = 50,000
─────────────────────────────────
Ticket revenue: 1,162,500

+ Sponsor: 800,000 (Title 1 + Gold 2 + Silver 4)
+ Merch/F&B: 50,000
─────────────────────────────────
Total revenue: 2,012,500 (cover cost 2.5x — margin 60%)
```

## Sponsor Pitch Tier

### Standard 5-Tier

| Tier | THB | Benefits |
|------|-----|----------|
| **Title** | 500k-2M | Naming "X presented by Sponsor" + Keynote 30min + Booth 6m + 50 ticket + Logo everywhere |
| **Diamond** | 200-500k | Breakout 30min + Booth 4m + 20 ticket + Logo prominent |
| **Gold** | 80-200k | Booth 3m + 10 ticket + Logo standard |
| **Silver** | 30-80k | 5 ticket + Logo small |
| **In-kind** | service-based | Logo + thanks (เช่น เครื่องดื่ม / ของแจก) |

### Sponsor Decision Drivers
1. **Audience match** — demographic + intent
2. **Brand affinity** — co-brand fit
3. **Lead generation** — booth + scan
4. **Content production** — co-content + speaker spot
5. **Hospitality** — VIP dinner + 1-on-1

### Pitch Deck (10 slide)
1. Cover + event overview
2. Last year stats (or projections)
3. This year theme + audience
4. Audience demographics (5 chart)
5. Sponsor benefit summary
6. Tier package detail
7. Past sponsor case study
8. Marketing reach (paid + earned + organic)
9. Pricing + deadline + bonus
10. Contact + sign next step

## Email Sequence (8 Email)

### Email 1 — Save-the-date (D-90)
**Subject:** "Mark your calendar — <event> [date]"
**Body:** Theme + key speaker tease + early signup link

### Email 2 — Speaker reveal #1 (D-75)
**Subject:** "Speaker drop: 3 names you don't want to miss"

### Email 3 — Early-bird launch (D-60)
**Subject:** "Early-bird OPEN — save 40% เฉพาะ 2 อาทิตย์"

### Email 4 — Early-bird closing (D-46)
**Subject:** "48 ชม. สุดท้าย — early-bird ปิด"

### Email 5 — Speaker reveal #2 + agenda (D-30)
**Subject:** "Full agenda + 5 new speakers added"

### Email 6 — Final push (D-14)
**Subject:** "Last 30 seats — ปิดสมัครศุกร์นี้"

### Email 7 — Logistics (D-3)
**Subject:** "Everything you need to know — [event] in 3 days"
**Body:** Parking, dress code, agenda PDF, contact, app

### Email 8 — Post-event recap (D+3)
**Subject:** "Thanks + photos + slides + next event"
**Body:** Recap + content link + NPS link + early-bird next year

## Post-Event ROI Formula

```
Total revenue = Ticket + Sponsor + Merch + Lead value
Total cost = Venue + AV + F&B + Speaker + Marketing + Staff

ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100

For B2B lead gen:
Lead value = MQL count × Avg deal size × Win rate × Margin

Content multiplier:
- Photo: 50-200 shot
- Video: 1 highlight + 5-10 clip series
- Article: 3-5 recap + insight
- Quote/clip: 20-30 social card
- 1 event = 6 months content
```

## Common Mistakes

1. ตั้ง KGI ไม่ชัด → ทุกอย่าง mediocre
2. Pricing ไม่มี anchor → standard ดูแพง
3. Speaker reveal พร้อมกันหมด → ไม่มี momentum
4. ลืม email D-3 → no-show 40-50%
5. ไม่มี contingency 15% → over budget
6. AV ไม่ test ก่อนงาน → audio fail = reputation พัง
7. Post-event content ปล่อยช้า > 14 วัน → engagement หาย
