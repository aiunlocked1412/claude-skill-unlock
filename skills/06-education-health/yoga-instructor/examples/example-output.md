## 💪 Standing Sequence — Hip Focus (20 min)

### Pose 1: Crescent Lunge (Anjaneyasana) — high lunge
- **Hold:** 5 breaths each side
- **Cue:** front knee 90°, back leg engaged + lifted, arms up
- **Mod:** back knee down (low lunge)

### Pose 2: Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)
- **Hold:** 8 breaths each side
- **Cue:** front thigh parallel, back foot 90°, gaze over front fingers
- **Mod:** shorter stance, hand on hip

### Pose 3: Side Angle (Utthita Parsvakonasana)
- **Hold:** 5 breaths each side
- **Cue:** front elbow on thigh OR hand to floor (block), top arm reach
- **Mod:** elbow on thigh + top hand on hip

### Pose 4: Triangle (Trikonasana)
- **Hold:** 5 breaths each side
- **Cue:** **NEVER hand on knee** — shin or block
- **Mod:** block, shorter stance

### Pose 5: Wide-leg fold (Prasarita Padottanasana A)
- **Hold:** 8 breaths
- **Cue:** feet parallel, fold from hips, hands frame head or block
- **Mod:** block under head, shorter stance

## 🎯 Peak Pose: Bakasana (Crow) — 12 min

### Why this pose for hip opening theme?
Bakasana ต้องเปิดสะโพก (knees come into upper arms) + draw knees toward armpits = **deep hip flexion + outer hip squeeze**

### Setup (5 min) — Prep poses

#### Prep 1: Garland (Malasana) — squat
- **Hold:** 1 min
- เปิดสะโพก inner + outer
- **Cue:** sit bones drop, elbows push knees apart
- **Mod:** block under sit bones, blanket under heels

#### Prep 2: Lizard (Utthan Pristhasana)
- **Hold:** 5 breaths each side
- Deep hip flexor stretch + outer hip
- **Mod:** back knee down, forearms on block

#### Prep 3: Boat (Navasana)
- **Hold:** 3 sets × 5 breaths
- Build core for arm balance
- **Mod:** hold thighs (knees bent)

### Peak: Bakasana entry (5 min)

**Step-by-step:**
1. Start in Malasana (squat)
2. Place hands on mat, shoulder-width, fingers spread
3. Bend elbows slightly (chaturanga arms)
4. Place knees onto upper arms (high — near armpit)
5. Shift weight forward — feel feet getting light
6. Lift one foot, then other (or both)
7. Hold 3-5 breaths
8. Lower with control

### Modifications (3 levels)

🟢 **Beginner:**
- Use block under feet (start higher)
- Lift only 1 foot at a time
- Keep gaze forward (not down — protect neck)

🟡 **Intermediate:**
- Both feet up
- Hold 5 breaths
- Try entering from Malasana directly

🔴 **Advanced:**
- Bakasana → Chaturanga (jump back)
- Eka Pada Bakasana (one-leg crow)
- Bakasana → Tripod headstand

### Counter pose (2 min)
- Child's pose (Balasana) — knees wide, forehead down
- Wrist circles + stretch

- **Hold:** 2 min each side

- **Cue:** square hips, fold forward if comfortable

- **Hold:** 1-2 min

- **Cue:** knees wide, ankles aligned, lower forearms down

- **Hold:** 3 min
- Deep inner hip + relaxation
- **Setup:** bolster under spine, blocks under knees

- **Hold:** 1 min each side

## 🌬️ Pranayama (5 min)

**Technique:** Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril) × 5 rounds

**Setup:**
- Sukhasana, spine tall
- Right hand: thumb, ring + pinky finger
- Left hand: relaxed in lap

**Pattern (1 round):**
- Close right nostril → inhale left 4 count
- Close both → hold 4 count
- Open right → exhale right 4 count
- Inhale right 4 count
- Hold 4 count
- Exhale left 4 count

**Cue:**
> "ถ้ารู้สึกตื่นเต้นกับการนับ ปล่อยทิ้ง — แค่หายใจสบาย slow + smooth"

**Effect:** balance สมองซ้าย-ขวา, calm nervous system

## 🌙 Savasana (10 min)

### Setup (1 min)
- นอนหงาย
- Bolster under knees (release lower back)
- Blanket cover ทั้งตัว (อุณหภูมิห้องลด)

## ⚠️ Contraindications + Modifications

### 🤰 Pregnancy
- **Skip:** Bakasana (peak), Frog (deep), prone backbends
- **Modify:** ใช้ chair-supported squat แทน Malasana, pigeon ด้วย bolster หนา

### 🦴 Lower back / SI joint issues
- **Skip:** Frog
- **Modify:** Pigeon → use bolster generously

### ✋ Wrist injury
- **Modify:** Chaturanga → knees down lower
- **Skip:** Bakasana — sub Boat pose

### ❤️ High BP / Heart
- **Skip:** any inversions (down dog OK, headstand no)
- **Modify:** keep head above heart in poses

### 🤕 Knee injury
- **Skip:** Frog, deep Pigeon
- **Modify:** less depth, more support

## 💡 Teaching Notes (สำหรับครู)

### What to emphasize
- "Hip opening" is more emotional than physical for many — be ready for emotional release in Pigeon
- Don't push Bakasana — falling = part of learning
- Soften your voice in cool-down

### Common student errors
- ❌ Triangle: hand on knee → 🟢 shin or block
- ❌ Bakasana: gaze down → neck strain → 🟢 gaze 1 ft in front
- ❌ Pigeon: hip lifts off ground → 🟢 use bolster

### Cueing tips
- Use short cues during flow (1-3 words: "inhale rise")
- Use longer cues during holds
- Use silence in Savasana

### Music suggestion
- Warm-up: instrumental, soft drum
- Peak: tribal beat (motivational)
- Cool-down + Savasana: ambient or silence
